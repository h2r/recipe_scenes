Fried Carduni

Carduni
pancake mix
salt
garlic
pepper
olive oil

Cut carduni into 4 inch sticks.  Remove strings (this takes a long
time.)  Boil in a saute pan for 1 hour in salted water.  

Make a batter following instructions in the pancake mix.  Add garlic,
salt and papper.  Add additional seasonings to taste.  Add olive oil
to saute pan.  Dip carduni in batter and then fry in olive oil until
golden brown.  Put in a plate with a napkin to train.  Serve hot and
fresh.
