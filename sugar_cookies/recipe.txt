Grandma's Sugar Cookies

1 cup butter
1 egg
2 1/2 cups flouir
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla

Preheat oven to 350.

Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg and vanilla.  Gradually blend in flour.  Chill dough it's easier to handle (1 hr or more).  Roll on lightly floured surface and cut your favorite shapes with cutters.
